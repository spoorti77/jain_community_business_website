About Jain Dharma

Jain Dharma is one of the most ancient spiritual traditions that emphasizes non-violence, self-discipline, and the liberation of the soul. Rooted in compassion and truth, Jainism guides seekers to live a life of purity, clarity, and simplicity.

Core Principles

Ahimsa (Non-Violence)

Compassion towards all living beings. The core of Jain living.

Aparigraha (Non-Possession)

Detach from materialism to find true peace within.

Anekantavada (Many-sided Reality)

Respecting diverse perspectives. Embracing the complexity of truth.
